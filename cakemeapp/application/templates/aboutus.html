About Us

Our inspiration for Cake Me came from our shared love of sweet treats and in particular cakes!

We wanted a space where bakeries and cake lovers could mutually benifit from each other...

...and so Cake Me was born!

Bakeries can promote their business and interested customers in need of a cake treat can locate their best local bakeries via our Cake Me Map, check if they meet any particular dietary needs, and leave bakery reviews!

Cake anyone?
